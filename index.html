About the author

I'm a software engineer from Finland who has been passionate about games and game development my whole life. I live in Kajaani, which is a small town in the middle of nowhere in the Kainuu region of Finland. I studied game development at Kajaani University of Applied Sciences and graduated in 2019. Ever since, I have done small game projects, most of them with Godot Engine or Unreal Engine.
