Welcome!

Greetings, and welcome to read my game development devlog. On this website, I will publish development logs of how my game project is ongoing.

For now, this devlog only focuses on a top-down shooter called Enfora Star, but more about the project is on the devlog page!

I hope you enjoy the posts and other content! Thanks for joining!

Best Regards, TheAspen

Enfora Star is an arcade-like top-down shooter. Collect new weapons or upgrade existing ones to fight better against enemy waves.

About the author

I'm a software engineer from Finland who has been passionate about games and game development my whole life. I live in Kajaani, which is a small town in the middle of nowhere in the Kainuu region of Finland. I studied game development at Kajaani University of Applied Sciences and graduated in 2019. Ever since, I have done small game projects, most of them with Godot Engine or Unreal Engine.
